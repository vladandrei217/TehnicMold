Why Tehnic Mold Trade?

Tradition. Experience. Quality. Professionalism.

Our Story

Since the beginning of 1999, Mold-Masters Ltd, a global leader in the production of hot runner systems for injection molds, has designated us as representatives in Romania. This strategic decision comes in response to the growing interest in this advanced technology locally. During this time, Mold-Masters products have been successfully integrated into the projects of the largest companies in the country, confirming the brand's tradition through premium quality, durability, and efficiency.

Market Recognition for Top Technological Solutions

One goal we strive to achieve is to be recognized in the market for the technological solutions offered, not merely as distributors of processing equipment. Thus, we have become, over time, the technological partner of the largest processors in Romania, providing solutions for applications dedicated to the injection processing of polymer materials.

Expanding the Range of Solutions through Strategic Partnerships

At the beginning of 2002, a new business opportunity was identified within the same market. To expand the range of solutions offered to our clients, we began exclusive representation for Romania of VEGA, the Italian manufacturer of hydraulic cylinders for injection molds. Additionally, to complete the range of high-tech mold components, in 2003 we became the representative for the Spanish company CUMSA.

Exclusive Representation of VEGA and CUMSA in Romania

Although the need for service interventions for Mold-Masters hot runners arises rarely, in order to minimize production downtime for our clients, we inaugurated the service center in 2015. In 2017, we began collaboration with DME, Tirad, and EXAflow to expand our portfolio of high-tech mold components dedicated to our clients and to improve their presence in Romania together.

Strengthening Our Position in the Local Market through Quality Partnerships

Over the years, our collaborations have strengthened our position in the local market, allowing us to offer our clients high-quality technological solutions. We are proud to represent these leading global companies and contribute to the integration of their products into the notable projects of companies in Romania. With a small but experienced team and an extensive range of products, we aim to remain a trusted partner for our clients, contributing to success and innovation in the polymer processing industry.

Commitment to Our Clients' Success and Innovation in the Polymer Industry

We are dedicated to being a trusted partner for our clients, providing top technological solutions that support success and innovation. Collaborations with global leaders enable us to deliver high-quality products and services, customized to optimize production processes and reduce downtime. Our experienced team ensures continuous support, actively contributing to the achievement of our partners' business objectives.
